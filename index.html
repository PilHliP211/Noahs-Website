Bio

Psychedelic blues from the Gulf Coast.

Noah Desimone & The Revival is Pensacola, Florida's premier psychedelic blues act. After performing in Blue Levee for nearly ten years, Noah Desimone is proud to continue electrifying Florida audiences with a unique blend of funk, acid rock, and blues.
